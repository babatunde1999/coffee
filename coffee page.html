☕ Behind the Beans

Every great cup of coffee starts with a story. At Coffee, we go beyond the brew. Our beans are handpicked from the finest farms across the globe, from the lush hills of Ethiopia to the highlands of Colombia. We build direct relationships with farmers who share our passion for quality, sustainability, and ethical sourcing. From harvesting to roasting, every step is crafted with care to bring out the unique flavor notes in each bean bold, rich, and unforgettable. We roast in small batches to preserve freshness and flavor, ensuring your cup tells the story of where it came from.

☕ Brewing Guide

French Press

Bold, rich, and full-bodied. Steep for 4 minutes using coarse grounds.

Pour Over

Clean, crisp, and balanced. Use medium grounds and pour in slow circles.

Espresso

Strong and concentrated. Brew in 25–30 seconds with fine grounds.

Cold Brew

Smooth and refreshing. Steep for 12–24 hours with coarse grounds.
